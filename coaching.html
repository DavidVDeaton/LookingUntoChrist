Dennis R Deaton is co-founder of Quma Learning Systems and an internationally known corporate educator and motivational speaker. He received his BA from the University of Utah and his DMD degree, cum laude, from Washington University in St. Louis.

He is also co-founder of Grand Key Education which provides online mindset and social-emotional training for teens.

He has authored:

  • Money: An Owner’s Manual
  • The Book on Mind Management
  • The Ownership Spirit: The One Grand Key That Changes Everything Else
  • Looking Unto Christ In Every Thought: Defeating Fear, Doubt & Discouragement

Dennis has taught numerous Adult Education courses for the Church Education System, and was a regular speaker at BYU Education Week for 22 years.

He and his wife Susan are the parents of nine children, and 30 grandchildren; and he currently serves as a stake patriarch and temple worker.

On a limited basis, Dr. Deaton accepts clients for individual coaching. Small group sessions are also available.

Ann Ferguson is a Master Mentor. Her passion is to teach spiritual principles and tools that heal emotional wounds. This helps people discover how to step into the belief that moves mountains and creates miracles. She loves to celebrate each step with those she mentors as they experience a divine partnership with Jesus Christ.

Ann also enjoys teaching others the art of mentoring and has trained and certified over 30 Joyful Heart Mentors.

Ann is the mother of 5 children. And a grandmother of 4 beautiful granddaughters. She has been teaching and mentoring for the past 25 years and loves a good adventure. Whether that is in diving into the heart, or diving into a cenote in Mexico. She is always ready to explore and experience the good things this world has to offer.

Ann is the owner of Joy Power, LLC. And has written 3 mentor-led courses you can experience at JoyfulHeartMentoring.com.

Ann teaches in small (usually 5-8 participants) mentored classes. She gives additional support throughout the week via social messaging apps. She meets one-on-one, when needed, to help you apply the skills and tools you are learning into your everyday life so that they become second nature.

April Price is a certified life coach who specializes in helping people who love God love their lives and thrive. While we believe in a plan of happiness, most of us spend our lives feeling pretty miserable and, for the most part, that misery is being created by our brains. April’s coaching focuses on showing you how to use your agency to manage your brain and direct your thoughts so you can get the most joy and fulfillment out of your earth life experience.

April is the owner of April Price Coaching, LLC and hosts the 100% Awesome Podcast.

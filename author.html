AUTHOR

About

Dennis R Deaton is co-founder of Quma Learning Systems and an internationally known corporate educator and motivational speaker. He received his BA from the University of Utah and his DMD degree, cum laude, from Washington University in St. Louis.

He is also co-founder of Grand Key Education which provides online mindset and social-emotional training for teens.

He has authored:

  • Money: An Owner’s Manual
  • The Book on Mind Management
  • The Ownership Spirit: The One Grand Key That Changes Everything Else

Dennis has taught numerous Adult Education courses for the Church Education System, and was a regular speaker at BYU Education Week for 22 years.

He and his wife Susan are the parents of nine children, and 30 grandchildren; and he currently serves as a stake patriarch and temple worker.

Purpose

Over a period of a few months, the author Dennis R Deaton felt whisperings of the Spirit urging him to write a book for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in which he would distill his life’s work, writings, and church experience into a book dealing with the adversary’s meddlings and interference with our thoughts.

LOOKING UNTO CHRIST IN EVERY THOUGHT WILL:

Alert you to the prevalence of Satan’s ploys and the specific tactics he uses against you.

Provide you with the mental skills to unmask “the liar within” and arm you with tools to repel him.

Encourage you to put greater trust in the promises of the Lord and strengthen your faith in obtaining them.

Inspire you to sanctify the Lord God in your heart by looking unto Christ in every thought.
